I created a website that saves you money using rounding errors.

Example: Simple loan.

  1. It calculates the interest you owe on a simple loan
  2. Then it shows you how much you can borrow for the same amount of interest by taking advantage of rounding.

It takes advantage of the way we round down fractional pennies until reaching half a penny (then we round up).

For example 5% of a penny is blah. So it incrementally adds pennies to your loan until it finds the amount before your interest rounds up to the next penny.

This applies to any transaction where you're charged a fee based on an interest rate, like credit card balance transfers, credit card cash advances and currency exchanges.

The amount it adds is based on the percentage interest rate and is always less than a dollar.
