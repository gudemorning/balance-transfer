Balance % Calculate

Credit card balance transfer rounding errors

Have you ever wondered how much more you can transfer between credit cards without paying more fees?

This tool figures it out for you.

For example

If you transfer a $100 credit card balance for a 10% fee...

You will pay a $10 fee.

But you can also transfer $100.04 for the same $10 fee!

You will pay $10.01 to transfer $100.05, however.
